🔍 Smart Money Concepts
  • Order Blocks — last bearish/bullish candle before impulse move
  • FVG / Imbalance — gap between candle wicks (magnet zones)
  • Liquidity Sweep — stop hunt above highs / below lows before reversal
  • BOS / CHOCH — break of structure = trend confirmation
  • Premium / Discount — above/below 50% of last swing = value zone
📊 Hidden Divergences
  • Regular Bull Div — price LL, RSI HL → reversal up
  • Regular Bear Div — price HH, RSI LH → reversal down
  • Hidden Bull Div — price HL, RSI LL → continuation up
  • Hidden Bear Div — price LH, RSI HH → continuation down
  • MACD Divergence — histogram diverging = early warning
🎯 Key Level Types
  • Weekly / Monthly S/R — highest TF = strongest levels
  • Round Numbers — $10k, $50k, $100k act as psychological magnets
  • Previous ATH / ATL — often become strong S/R after breach
  • 0.618 / 0.786 Fib — golden pocket = prime retracement buy zone
  • Anchored VWAP — institutions use AVWAP from major swing points
📈 Volume Profile
  • POC — most traded price = magnet zone
  • VAH / VAL — 70% of volume traded within this range
  • Low Volume Node — thin areas = price moves fast through
  • High Volume Node — thick areas = price finds S/R
  • Volume Delta — buy vs sell pressure per candle
⏰ Multi-Timeframe Checklist
  • Monthly / Weekly — macro trend & major S/R zones
  • Daily — directional bias, confirm structure
  • 4H — entry model zone, OB + FVG
  • 1H / 15m — fine-tune entry, look for CHOCH
  • Rule: trade WITH higher TF, enter on lower TF
